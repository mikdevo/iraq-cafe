Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Discord server?

Click the Discord link above or use the invite code: discord.gg/iraq. Make sure to read our rules and introduce yourself in the welcome channel!

How do I get server information?

Server IPs and connection details are available in our Discord server. Join our Discord to get the latest server information and updates.

How does the Iraq Cafè Dinar store work?

Our store uses Iraq Cafè Dinar (ICD) as virtual currency. You can purchase Discord perks, roles, and merchandise. Contact our staff for more information.

How do I report a problem?

You can report issues through our Discord server, contact our staff directly, or use the contact form above. We take all reports seriously and respond quickly.
