About Iraq Cafè

Discover our community's story and mission

Our Mission

Iraq Cafè is more than just a Discord server - it's a digital gathering place where Iraqi culture, gaming, and community come together. We celebrate our heritage while embracing modern technology and global friendships.

Our Story

Founded with the vision of creating a welcoming space for Iraqis and friends worldwide, Iraq Cafè has grown into a vibrant community that bridges cultures through gaming, conversation, and shared experiences.

Our Values

Respect & Inclusivity

We treat every member with dignity and respect, creating an inclusive environment for all.

Cultural Celebration

We proudly celebrate Iraqi culture and traditions while embracing diversity.

Gaming Passion

We share a deep passion for gaming and building strong communities.

Global Friendship

We build lasting friendships that transcend borders and cultures.

What Makes Us Special

Active Community

Our community is always buzzing with activity. From daily conversations to gaming sessions, there's always something happening. Members from around the world come together to share experiences, stories, and create lasting friendships.

Professional Moderation

Our dedicated staff ensures a safe and welcoming environment for everyone. With 24/7 moderation, we maintain the highest standards of community management while fostering positive interactions.

Regular Events

Join our exciting tournaments, competitions, and community events. From gaming tournaments to cultural celebrations, we host events that bring our community together and create unforgettable memories.

Game Servers

Play on our dedicated Minecraft and Squad servers with friends from around the world. Our servers are optimized for the best gaming experience with active moderation and community events.
