Community Rules

Please read and follow these rules to maintain a positive environment

1

No Swearing or Cussing

Keep conversations respectful and family-friendly

Swearing and/or cussing isn't allowed in our community. We maintain a family-friendly environment where everyone feels comfortable. This includes:

  • No profanity or offensive language
  • No inappropriate jokes or comments
  • Keep discussions respectful and constructive
2

Respect Each Other

Treat all members with kindness and dignity

Respecting each other is mandatory in our community. This means:

  • Treat everyone with kindness and respect
  • No harassment, bullying, or discrimination
  • Listen to others' opinions and perspectives
  • Be patient and understanding with new members
3

No Unrelated Problems

Keep discussions relevant to the server

Don't cause troubles and/or problems that aren't related to the server. This includes:

  • Keep personal issues outside the server
  • Don't bring drama from other communities
  • Stay on topic in relevant channels
  • Use appropriate channels for different discussions
4

No Role Requests

Roles are given based on merit and contribution

Don't ask for any role or promotion. Our staff carefully considers role assignments based on:

  • Active participation in the community
  • Positive contributions and helpfulness
  • Following server rules consistently
  • Demonstrating leadership qualities
5

No Spamming

Keep messages meaningful and avoid repetition

Spamming is forbidden in all its ways. This includes:

  • No excessive messages or repeated content
  • No self-promotion without permission
  • No off-topic content in wrong channels
  • No excessive use of emojis or reactions
6

No Sensitive Topics

Keep discussions neutral and inclusive

Don't talk about topics that include politics, religion, or racism. We avoid:

  • Political discussions and debates
  • Religious arguments or proselytizing
  • Racist, sexist, or discriminatory content
  • Controversial topics that may cause division
